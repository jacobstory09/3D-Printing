Terrain viewer

KML boundary → USGS 3DEP + overhead imagery (UTM). Use satellite or ortho for trees, water, roofs. Drag to orbit · scroll to zoom.

Export terrain.glb (textured surface — no print base) terrain_obj.zip (same surface + texture) terrain_print.stl (Z-up, XY bed) terrain_print.glb (Z-up, same solid) terrain_print.3mf (Z-up, color-capable) terrain_print_pieces.zip (puzzle STLs)
Preview and exports share one frame: X east, Y north, Z elevation (m). WebGL uses Z-up (camera up); print STLs are the same axes in mm (XY bed, Z height). terrain.glb is centered in XY. Satellite: Esri, Maxar, Earthstar, GIS community. OSM © contributors. OAM = dataset license.
